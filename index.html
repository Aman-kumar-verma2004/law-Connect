      When to Find a Lawyer?

      Youll need to find a lawyer for your big moments in life, including:

      • Resolving a divorce or other family law matters, including binding financial agreements, separation, custody of children and property settlement, with a family lawyer.
      • Defending a criminal charge or traffic charge from the police in court with a criminal lawyer or traffic lawyer.
      • Making a Will and power of attorney to give you and your family peace of mind with the help of a wills and estate planning lawyer.
      • Buying or selling a home or investment property with the help of a property lawyer.
      • Starting, running, buying or selling a business with the help of a business lawyer.
      • Getting Australian citizenship or a visa to live, work or study in Australia with the help of an immigration lawyer.
      • Making a personal injury compensation claim after a serious accident with the help of a personal injury lawyer.

      How to Find a Lawyer?

      You can quickly search and find a lawyer (or a registered migration agent, notary public or licensed conveyancer) by location so that you can speak with or meet a lawyer who is close to your home or work.

      You can also search by area of law so that you can get the right legal advice for your circumstances - from Business Law to Family Law and Personal Injury.

      Once you've found a lawyer, you can instantly book online with an available lawyer who has verified client reviews so that you can make an informed choice.

      Plus email and SMS booking confirmation and reminders are all included, so you have your appointment information at your fingertips.
